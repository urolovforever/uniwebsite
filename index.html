About Tashkent International University

Founded with a vision to provide world-class education, TIU stands as one of Central Asia's leading international universities. We combine academic excellence with innovation, preparing students to become global leaders and changemakers.

Our diverse community of students and faculty from over 50 countries creates a truly international learning environment. With strong partnerships with leading universities worldwide, TIU offers unparalleled opportunities for academic and personal growth.
