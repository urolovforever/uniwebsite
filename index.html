Welcome to Tashkent International University

Tashkent International University is a premier institution of higher education committed to providing world-class education and fostering innovation, research, and leadership. We combine academic excellence with practical experience to prepare students for successful careers in a globalized world. With over 10,000 students, 500+ expert faculty members, and partnerships with 100+ universities worldwide, TIU is your gateway to international opportunities.
