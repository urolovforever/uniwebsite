Joint Degrees

Earn two internationally recognized degrees through our partnership programmes with leading universities worldwide.

Two degrees. One journey.

TIU's joint degree programmes allow you to study at two universities and graduate with two fully recognized diplomas. Our partnerships are built on shared academic standards, so credits transfer seamlessly between institutions.

Joint degrees are available at both bachelor's and master's level. You'll benefit from the academic strengths of two institutions, experience different cultures and teaching approaches, and build an international professional network — all before you graduate.

Each programme is designed so that your studies at both universities complement each other, giving you deeper expertise and broader perspectives in your field.

How joint degrees work

A step-by-step overview of your journey from application to graduation with two degrees.

1

Apply to TIU

Submit your application to TIU and indicate your interest in a joint degree programme during the admissions process.

2

Study at TIU

Complete your first two years at TIU's Tashkent campus, building a strong academic foundation in English.

3

Transfer abroad

Move to a partner university to complete the remaining two years of your programme in a new country and culture.

4

Graduate with two degrees

Receive diplomas from both TIU and the partner university — fully recognized in Uzbekistan and internationally.
