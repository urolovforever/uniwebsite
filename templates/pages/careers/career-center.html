Career Center

Your one-stop destination for career guidance, job search support, and professional development resources at TIU.

Get job ready from day one

Whatever your goals, we're here to help you take the next step with confidence.

At the University of Hull, you'll receive personalised careers advice, opportunities to gain experience, and support to boost your employability.

Employability is embedded in your course, and there are plenty of ways to get involved in workshops, events, and one-to-one guidance.

Whether you're looking for part-time work, your first graduate job, or exploring your options, we're with you every step of the way.

Career mentoring

Get advice from someone who's been there. Our mentoring programme connects you with alumni and industry professionals for advice, support and encouragement as you explore your career options.

Skills workshops

Get practical support to feel career ready. From writing strong CVs and applications to interview preparation, our workshops give you the tools to succeed - whatever stage you're at.

Work placements

Gain hands-on experience in your field with structured work placements. Build your professional network and develop the skills employers are looking for.

Employer connections

Meet employers at careers fairs, networking events and on-campus talks. Build relationships with organisations looking to recruit talented graduates like you.
