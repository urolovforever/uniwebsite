That's where you come in.

How to recruit one of our students or graduates

We offer local, regional and national employers the opportunity to advertise full or part-time jobs, casual work, internships and placements through the Handshake platform.

Maintaining an active profile on Handshake is quick and easy and will enhance your visibility as an employer of choice for our students and graduates. It is also completely free of charge and allows you to:

  • Advertise unlimited vacancies to students and graduates from the University of Hull.
  • Increase brand awareness amongst our student body through your digital employer profile.
  • Send targeted communications to our students and graduates to promote your opportunities.
  • Add details of online or in person careers events.

You can create a Handshake user account online, with useful information on using the system also available here.

Our students and graduates actively use the system but our Employer Engagement Team can work closely with you to maximise engagement and advise on which of our students or courses might be most suited to your needs.

Other opportunities to speak to us about

Across the University, students from a wide range of courses are offered the chance to take part in shorter placement programmes and live projects. We are always interested in hearing from employers interested in getting involved so please don't hesitate to get in touch.

Share your industry experience by becoming a mentor to our students. Our mentoring programmes connect professionals with students to provide guidance on career development, industry insights, and professional skills.

Attend our regular careers fairs and employer events to meet students face-to-face, showcase your organisation, and identify potential talent for your business.

Advertise part-time, casual and seasonal work opportunities to our students. Many students are looking for flexible work that fits around their studies.

Collaborate with our students on real-world academic projects. Provide a brief or challenge and benefit from fresh perspectives while students gain valuable practical experience.
