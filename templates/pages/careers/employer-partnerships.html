Employer Partnerships

Connect with the next generation of talented, multilingual professionals. Partner with TIU to recruit exceptional graduates.

Why Recruit From TIU?

Tashkent International University produces graduates who are not only academically excellent but also professionally prepared. Our curriculum integrates real-world projects, international standards, and industry partnerships to ensure students are job-ready from day one.

Our students are fluent in multiple languages including Uzbek, Russian, English, and often a fourth language. They bring diverse perspectives, strong analytical skills, and a global mindset to every organization they join.

With programs spanning Business & Economics, IT & Engineering, Law, and Humanities, we offer a broad talent pool across disciplines. Our Career Center team works directly with employers to understand hiring needs and match the right candidates.

What Sets Us Apart

International Curriculum

Programs aligned with global standards, accredited internationally, and taught by faculty from leading universities worldwide.

Multilingual Graduates

Students are proficient in at least three languages, making them ideal for multinational teams and global operations.

Industry-Ready Skills

Capstone projects, internships, and hands-on training ensure graduates can contribute from their first day on the job.

Proven Track Record

92% of our graduates are employed within six months, many at top companies in Central Asia and beyond.

Partnership Opportunities

We offer flexible partnership models designed to meet your recruitment goals, whether you are a startup looking for interns or a multinational corporation seeking top graduates.

Each partnership tier includes dedicated support from our employer relations team, access to our student database, and branding opportunities across campus.

Custom packages are also available for organizations with unique needs. Contact us to discuss a tailored partnership plan.

Job Board Access

Strategic Partnership

Co-develop curriculum, sponsor research projects, and gain priority access to top talent through our exclusive strategic partner program.
