Alumni Network

Stay connected after graduation — join a growing community of TIU alumni making an impact across the globe.

Once a TIU student, always part of the family.

Graduation doesn't mean goodbye. TIU's Alumni Network connects thousands of graduates working in Uzbekistan and internationally — in technology, business, law, education, government, and beyond.

As an alumnus, you gain access to career resources, networking events, mentorship opportunities, and exclusive job postings. You also have the chance to give back by mentoring current students, speaking at campus events, or supporting scholarships.

The network grows stronger every year, and so do the opportunities it creates for every member.

Alumni benefits

Your TIU connection keeps working for you long after you graduate.

Job board access

Browse exclusive job listings from TIU employer partners, posted directly to our alumni portal.

Networking events

Attend alumni mixers, industry panels, and homecoming events to reconnect and expand your professional circle.

Mentorship programme

Mentor current students or be mentored by senior alumni — building bridges across generations of TIU graduates.

Lifelong learning

Access discounted professional development courses, workshops, and certificate programmes at TIU.

Alumni card

Receive a TIU Alumni Card granting access to campus facilities, the library, sports center, and partner discounts.

Global chapters

Connect with TIU alumni in your city through regional chapters in South Korea, Turkey, Europe, and across Uzbekistan.

Get involved

There are many ways to stay connected and give back to the TIU community:

  • Become a mentor — guide current students through career decisions and professional development
  • Guest lecture — share your industry expertise with students in your former faculty
  • Refer talent — post job opportunities and internships from your company on the alumni portal
  • Support scholarships — contribute to the Alumni Scholarship Fund that supports the next generation of TIU students
