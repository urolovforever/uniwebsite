Schools and disciplines

Together, our Schools conduct industry-leading research tackling national and global challenges. We bring this passion and experience back to our students through a thriving portfolio of undergraduate, postgraduate, and professional programmes.

The School of IT & Engineering delivers programmes in computer science, software engineering, cybersecurity, and data science — with 2+2 pathways to KAIST and Riga Technical University.

The School of Business & Economics combines academic rigour with real-world application — case studies, internships, and partnerships with leading global employers.

PhD Programmes offer doctoral-level research in business, IT, engineering, and humanities — designed for aspiring researchers and academics.

The Data Science & AI programme equips students with skills in machine learning, big data analytics, and artificial intelligence — preparing them for the most in-demand careers in tech.

The Cybersecurity track covers network security, ethical hacking, and digital forensics — building expertise to protect organisations in an increasingly connected world.

Management & Entrepreneurship develops future business leaders through hands-on projects, startup incubation, and partnerships with global companies.

Research

Research in the Faculty of IT, Business, and Engineering is driving innovation across Central Asia. We're developing next-generation software systems, advancing data science methodologies, and building sustainable business models for emerging economies.

Our research community is pioneering work in artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, fintech, and smart infrastructure. We're collaborating with international partners to solve real-world challenges and translate academic insight into industry impact.
