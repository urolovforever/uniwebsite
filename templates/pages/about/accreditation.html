Our Quality Promise

Quality you can count on

Your TIU degree is officially recognized by the Ministry of Higher Education of Uzbekistan and validated by our international partner universities. Through our 2+2 dual degree partnerships, TIU programs align with the academic requirements of partner institutions abroad.

This means seamless credit transfer — no extra exams, no lost credits. We continuously review and improve our programs to stay aligned with global best practices in higher education.
