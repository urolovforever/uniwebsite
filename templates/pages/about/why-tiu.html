What Makes Us Different

Two degrees. Two countries. One journey.

TIU isn't just another university. We're the only institution in Uzbekistan offering a full 2+2 dual degree program — two years in Tashkent, two years at a partner university abroad. You graduate with two degrees, fluency in English, and an international network that follows you for life.

GOLD-STANDARD TEACHING

Teaching worth its weight in Gold

We're rated Gold in the latest Teaching Excellence Framework (TEF). What does that mean for you? It means you learn from the best, so you'll be at your best. And that means you'll have the best chance of achieving what you want in your life and career.
