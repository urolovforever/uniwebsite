Travel FAQs

Tashkent International University is approximately 20 minutes from Islam Karimov International Airport (TAS) by car or taxi. You can use Yandex Go or MyTaxi apps for a convenient ride directly to campus.

Yes, free parking is available on campus for both students and visitors. Designated visitor parking is located near the main administration building. Just follow the signs from the main entrance.

Absolutely. Multiple bus routes stop within a 5-minute walk of campus, and the nearest Tashkent Metro station is about 10 minutes away on foot. The metro is the fastest way to travel across the city.

High-speed Afrosiyob trains connect Tashkent to Samarkand (around 2 hours), Bukhara (around 4 hours), and other cities. From Tashkent Railway Station, you can reach campus in about 15 minutes by taxi or metro.

During orientation and Open Day events, TIU arranges shuttle services from key locations. For international students arriving by air, we can help coordinate airport pick-up through the International Office. Contact us in advance to arrange.

The most popular taxi apps in Tashkent are Yandex Go and MyTaxi. Both are affordable, reliable, and accept card payments. Simply enter "Tashkent International University" as your destination.
