Support FAQs

You can book a counseling session online through the student portal, email studentlife@tiu.uz, or visit the Student Affairs office during walk-in hours (Monday-Friday, 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM). All sessions are free and confidential.

Start by speaking with your course instructor or academic advisor. You can also visit the Academic Support Centre for tutoring, study skills workshops, and writing assistance. The library also offers research support sessions.

Contact IT Support by emailing it@tiu.uz or visiting the IT Help Desk on campus. For urgent issues during class hours, you can also call the IT hotline. Most issues are resolved within the same day.

Yes. The International Office provides full visa and immigration support for all international students, including document preparation, registration assistance, and renewal guidance. Contact international@tiu.uz for help.

TIU offers scholarships and financial aid options. If you're experiencing financial hardship, contact the Finance Office at finance@tiu.uz to discuss your situation. We also have an emergency fund for students facing unexpected circumstances.
