The Rector's Office

Your connection to university leadership

The Rector's Reception serves as the central point of contact between the university community and its leadership. Whether you're a student with a concern, a parent with a question, or a partner seeking collaboration — this is where conversations begin.

We believe in transparent, accessible leadership. The Reception team ensures your message reaches the right person, and that you receive a timely, thoughtful response.

How We Help

What the Rector's Reception handles

01

Appeals & Feedback

Submit formal appeals, complaints, or feedback about any aspect of university life. Every submission is reviewed and directed to the appropriate department for resolution.

02

Official Requests

Request official letters, references, or documents from the university. The Reception coordinates with academic and administrative offices to process your needs.

03

Meetings & Appointments

Schedule a meeting with the Rector or senior leadership. We coordinate calendars and ensure your time with university leaders is productive and focused.
