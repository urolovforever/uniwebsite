Global Pathway

Two countries. Two degrees. One incredible journey.

Our 2+2 dual degree programme is designed for students who think globally. Spend your first two years at TIU in Tashkent, immersing yourself in a multicultural campus. Then transfer to one of our partner universities in South Korea, Poland, Latvia, or beyond to complete your degree. You'll graduate with two internationally recognised qualifications and a truly global perspective.

ENGLISH-MEDIUM INSTRUCTION

Study in English from day one

Every lecture, every seminar, every assignment — delivered in English. Our international faculty come from universities across Europe, Asia, and North America, bringing diverse teaching styles and global academic standards. You won't need to learn Uzbek to succeed here, though you're welcome to pick it up along the way.

AFFORDABLE EDUCATION

World-class education without the world-class price tag

Tashkent offers one of the lowest costs of living among university cities in Asia. A full meal costs around $3, monthly rent starts from $200, and public transport is under $10 a month. Combined with competitive tuition fees and scholarship opportunities, TIU makes quality international education genuinely accessible.

VISA & STUDENT SUPPORT

We'll handle the paperwork. You focus on packing.

Our dedicated International Office guides you through every step — from visa applications and invitation letters to airport pickup and accommodation arrangements. We provide orientation programmes, buddy systems pairing you with local students, and 24/7 support to make sure you feel at home from the moment you arrive.

DISCOVER TASHKENT

An ancient city with a modern heartbeat

Tashkent is a city of contrasts — where 2,000-year-old Silk Road heritage meets gleaming metro stations, bustling bazaars sit next to modern shopping centres, and world-class museums are just a short walk from vibrant nightlife. With direct flights to dozens of international destinations, it's your gateway to exploring Central Asia and beyond.
