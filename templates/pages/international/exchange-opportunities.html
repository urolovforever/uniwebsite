Exchange Opportunities

Discover semester exchanges, summer schools, and international programmes available to TIU students.

Why come to TIU?

A proud history, academic excellence, and a destination filled with opportunity.

You'll be part of a diverse student community and have the chance to study and socialise on a safe and friendly campus that has everything you need in one place. If you want to explore further afield, Tashkent's historic Silk Road landmarks and vibrant bazaars await. And as one of the most affordable cities in Central Asia, your money goes further here.

Explore Central Asia while you study

Tashkent has great transport links making it easy to visit Samarkand, Bukhara, and other historic cities during your time with us.

Study in English from day one

All exchange courses are delivered in English. No Uzbek language requirement — though you're welcome to learn along the way.

Affordable student living

Tashkent is one of the most affordable student cities in Asia. A full meal costs around $3, and rent starts from $200 per month.

A welcoming international community

Join students from across Central Asia, South Korea, Turkey, Europe, and beyond. Our campus is built on diversity and inclusion.

Safe and supportive environment

Tashkent is ranked among the safest capitals in the region. Our International Office provides full support from arrival to departure.

Ways to experience TIU

Exchange student programme

If your institution has an exchange agreement with us, you can come to TIU as an international exchange student. You'll study alongside our degree students for one or two semesters, with full access to campus facilities and student life.

Visiting student programme

If your university does not have an exchange agreement, you can still apply directly to TIU as an international visiting student. Choose from a range of modules across our faculties and earn credits that transfer back to your home institution.

Not sure which applies to you?

Contact our team at international@tiu.uz and we'll be happy to help.

Exchange FAQs

For the Exchange Student Programme, yes — your home university must have an active exchange agreement with TIU. If there is no agreement, you can still apply through our Visiting Student Programme, which is open to students from any accredited university.

Exchange students can study at TIU for one semester (autumn or spring) or a full academic year. The duration depends on your home university's agreement with TIU and your own academic plan.

Exchange students continue to pay tuition to their home university — no additional tuition is charged by TIU. Visiting students may be subject to a separate fee; contact the International Office for details.

Our International Office provides full support — from visa and accommodation assistance to airport pickup, orientation, and a buddy programme pairing you with a local student. You'll have access to all campus facilities, student clubs, and events.

For the autumn semester, apply by 1 May. For the spring semester, apply by 1 November. Applications are submitted through your home university's exchange coordinator, who will liaise with our International Office.
