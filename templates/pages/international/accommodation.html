Accommodation

Find your home in Tashkent — from university housing to private rentals, we'll help you settle in.

Home sweet Tashkent

This isn't just a place to stay — it's where your university journey begins. The late-night study sessions, the friendships that last a lifetime, the moments that shape who you'll become. Living near campus puts you right at the heart of it all, just minutes from lectures, the library, and student life.

Your room: guaranteed

International students accepted to TIU are guaranteed accommodation support. Apply by 1 August in your year of entry to secure your place in university-arranged housing.

Whether you're looking for a shared student apartment or a private studio, TIU's International Office will help you find the right option. Rent in Tashkent starts from just $200/month — making it one of the most affordable student cities in Asia.

University Student Residence

FROM $200 P/M 10 OR 12 MONTHS
Affordable, social, convenient

TIU's partner student residence is the most popular choice for international students. Located just 10 minutes from campus, it offers fully furnished shared rooms, communal kitchens, laundry facilities, and 24/7 security. It's where most friendships start — and where you'll feel at home from day one.

Find out more about the residence

Private Studio Apartments

FROM $350 P/M 6 OR 12 MONTHS
Independence and privacy

For students who prefer their own space, TIU's International Office can help you find a private studio apartment near campus. These come fully furnished with a kitchen, bathroom, Wi-Fi, and air conditioning. Ideal for postgraduate students or those who value a quieter environment for focused study.

Explore private apartments

Shared Student Flats

FROM $150 P/M 10 OR 12 MONTHS
Budget-friendly, community living

Share a flat with fellow international or local students in the neighbourhoods around campus. Each student gets their own bedroom, with shared living room, kitchen, and bathroom. It's the most affordable option and a great way to experience Tashkent life while building lasting friendships.

Learn about shared flats

Our accommodation guarantee

All new international students accepted to TIU are guaranteed accommodation support through our International Office. Just as long as you apply by 1 August in your year of entry.

Whether you need help finding a student residence, a private apartment, or a shared flat — our team will guide you through every step, from booking to move-in day. Contact the International Office to get started.

Accommodation FAQs
