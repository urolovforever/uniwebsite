Publications

Explore the scholarly output of TIU's researchers — from peer-reviewed journal articles to conference proceedings and books.

Research that reaches the world.

TIU faculty and students publish in internationally recognized journals, present at global conferences, and contribute to edited volumes. Our publications span every discipline represented at the university — from IT and engineering to business, law, humanities, and education.

We encourage open-access publishing wherever possible, ensuring that our research is available to scholars, practitioners, and policymakers worldwide. TIU also publishes its own conference proceedings and working paper series.

The Research Office provides support for manuscript preparation, journal selection, peer review navigation, and publication funding.

Types of publications

TIU researchers contribute to a wide range of scholarly formats.

Journal articles

Peer-reviewed papers published in Scopus-indexed, Web of Science, and other internationally recognized academic journals.

Books & chapters

Monographs, textbooks, and contributed chapters in edited volumes published by international academic presses.

Conference proceedings

Papers presented at TIU's own conferences and at external academic events, collected in published proceedings volumes.

Working papers

Pre-publication research shared through TIU's working paper series, providing early access to new findings and inviting feedback.

Policy briefs

Short, accessible documents translating research findings into actionable recommendations for government and industry stakeholders.

Theses & dissertations

Master's theses and doctoral dissertations produced by TIU students, available through the university library repository.

Key research areas

Our publications span every faculty at TIU. The most active areas include:

  • Artificial Intelligence & Data Science
  • Economic Development in Central Asia
  • Legal Reform & Governance
  • Multilingual Education & Applied Linguistics
  • International Relations & Diplomacy
