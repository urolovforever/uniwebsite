Conferences

TIU hosts and participates in academic conferences that connect scholars, practitioners, and students across disciplines.

Share knowledge. Shape the future.

Academic conferences are central to TIU's research mission. They give our faculty and students a platform to present findings, receive peer feedback, and engage with international thought leaders.

Each year TIU organizes its own flagship conferences and supports faculty attendance at major international events. We believe that the exchange of ideas across borders is what drives genuine academic progress.

Conference participation is open to researchers at all levels — from undergraduate presenters to distinguished keynote speakers.

How to participate

Whether you want to present a paper, attend as a listener, or volunteer, here's how to get involved:

  • Submit an abstract — respond to the call for papers published on our events page before the deadline
  • Peer review — your abstract is reviewed by the scientific committee; accepted papers are scheduled for presentation
  • Register — all participants (presenters and attendees) must register through the conference portal
  • Publication — selected papers are published in TIU's conference proceedings or partner journals

For presenters

ABSTRACT LENGTH

250–300 words in English

PRESENTATION FORMAT

15-minute talk + 5-minute Q&A or poster session

REGISTRATION FEE

Free for TIU students and staff

LANGUAGE

English (Uzbek/Russian sessions available)
