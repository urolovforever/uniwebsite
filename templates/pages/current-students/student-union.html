What is the Student Union?

When you join TIU, you automatically become a member of the TIU Student Union (TIUSU).

TIUSU is here to ensure you feel part of the student community and help you look forward to a bright future. We do this by representing your academic interests and giving you a voice to speak out about the issues that matter to you most.

Getting involved in TIUSU is the best way to make friends, try new things, find your people and become part of something bigger.

Sardor Alimov President of Student Union 2025/26

For students, by students

TIUSU is independent from the University. It's led by a team of elected full- and part-time Student Leaders who work to represent students' views and improve university life for all. Students from any year and studying at any level can run for a Student Leader role.

Meet new people, become old friends

Joining a club, playing sports, or volunteering are the best ways to find your people. Choose from 15+ student clubs, sports teams, and creative societies — or start your own.

Where can I find TIUSU on campus?

You can't miss us. We're based in Building B, ground floor — but we run activities right across campus. You'll find the Student Union office just opposite the main cafeteria and next to the student lounge.
