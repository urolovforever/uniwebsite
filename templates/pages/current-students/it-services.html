What we provide

IT services for students

01

Campus Wi-Fi

High-speed wireless internet across all buildings, lecture halls, dormitories, and outdoor areas. Connect with your student credentials.

02

Student email

Every student receives a @tiu.uz email address with Microsoft 365 access — including Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and OneDrive.

03

Software licences

Free access to Microsoft Office 365, Adobe Creative Cloud, MATLAB, and other software through your student account.

04

Computer labs

Open-access labs with desktop computers, high-speed internet, and specialist software for coursework and projects.

05

IT Help Desk

On-campus support for password resets, connectivity issues, device setup, and software troubleshooting. Walk in or email us.

06

Cybersecurity

We protect the campus network and your data. Follow our guidelines to stay safe online — and report any suspicious activity.

How to connect to campus Wi-Fi

1

Open Wi-Fi settings and select TIU-Student

2

Enter your student ID as username and LMS password

3

Accept the security certificate when prompted

4

You're connected — your device will auto-join next time

Having trouble?

Visit the IT Help Desk in Building A, Room 105, or email it@tiu.uz. We're available Monday–Friday, 09:00–18:00.
