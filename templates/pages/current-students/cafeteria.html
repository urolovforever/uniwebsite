Cafeteria & Dining

Affordable meals, diverse menus, and a social space at the heart of campus life.

Eat well, study well

The TIU cafeteria serves fresh, affordable meals every day — from traditional Uzbek dishes to international options. It's also one of the most popular social spots on campus, where students catch up between classes over a cup of tea or a hot lunch.

All food is prepared daily by our on-site kitchen team using fresh, locally sourced ingredients.
