How to apply for dormitory housing

Follow these simple steps to secure your room. Applications open each year in July for the autumn semester.

01

Check eligibility

All enrolled TIU students are eligible. First-year and international students are given priority in the allocation process.

02

Submit application

Complete the online housing application form through your LMS student portal. You'll need your student ID and programme details.

03

Receive offer

Allocation results are sent by email within 2 weeks. You'll receive your room assignment, move-in date, and payment instructions.

04

Move in

Arrive on your assigned move-in date. Our housing team will greet you, hand over your key, and give you a quick tour of the building.
