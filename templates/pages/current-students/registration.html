Registration

Step-by-step guide to course registration, enrolment, and module selection.

Register on time. Stay on track.

Course registration is your opportunity to build your semester schedule. Each registration period, you select the modules you want to study, confirm your enrolment, and secure your place in classes.

Registration opens twice a year — before the fall and spring semesters. Late registration is possible within the first week of classes but is subject to availability and a late fee. We strongly recommend registering during the main registration window.

If you need help choosing modules, speak to your academic advisor or visit the Registrar's Office.

Need help registering?

Visit the Registrar's Office or contact your academic advisor for guidance on module selection.

How to register

Follow these steps to complete your course registration each semester.

1

Check your status

Log in to the student portal and confirm that you have no outstanding financial or academic holds on your account.

2

Choose modules

Browse available modules for your programme and year. Check prerequisites and timetable conflicts before selecting.

3

Submit registration

Add your chosen modules to the registration form and submit. You'll receive a confirmation email within 24 hours.

4

Attend classes

Once confirmed, your timetable is finalized. Attend all registered classes from the first day of the semester.

Important information

Keep these points in mind during the registration process:

  • You must clear all tuition balances before registration opens
  • Module changes (add/drop) are allowed during the first week of classes only
  • Some modules have limited seats — register early to secure your first choices
  • Check prerequisites — you cannot register for advanced modules without completing required courses
  • Late registration incurs a fee and is subject to seat availability

Registration dates

FALL SEMESTER

Main registration: 15–30 August

Late registration: 1–7 September

SPRING SEMESTER

Main registration: 15–31 January

Late registration: 1–7 February

ADD / DROP PERIOD

First 7 days of each semester
