{% trans "Core Principles" %}

{% trans "Lawfulness" %}

{% trans "All students must follow the laws of the Republic of Uzbekistan and the internal regulations of the university." %}

{% trans "Fairness and Integrity" %}

{% trans "Students are expected to act with honesty, fairness, and impartiality in all academic and personal interactions." %}

{% trans "Patriotism and Responsibility" %}

{% trans "Students carry out their academic duties based on moral values, loyalty to the country, and a commitment to their education." %}

{% trans "Efficiency and Innovation" %}

{% trans "Students are encouraged to continuously improve through innovative approaches and responsible use of university resources." %}

{% trans "Dress Code" %}

{% trans "All students must dress in a manner appropriate to the academic environment. The university requires a clean, modest, and professional appearance." %}

  • {% trans "Female students: light-colored blouses, knee-length skirts or classic trousers, dark shoes" %}
  • {% trans "Male students: white, light blue, or light gray shirts, classic trousers, dark shoes" %}
  • {% trans "Outerwear (coats, jackets, hats) must not be worn inside classrooms" %}
  • {% trans "Clothing must not display religious or subculture symbols" %}
  • {% trans "Weekends: casual dress is allowed (with exceptions for official events)" %}

{% trans "Not Allowed" %}

{% trans "Transparent, revealing, or overly tight clothing" %}

{% trans "Visible piercings or tattoos (except ears and fingers)" %}

{% trans "Unusual hair colors or unkempt appearance" %}

{% trans "Headphones worn in classrooms or hallways" %}

{% trans "Campus Behavior" %}

{% trans "Punctuality" %}

{% trans "Arrive to classes on time. Late arrival is not allowed without a valid reason and the instructor's permission." %}

{% trans "Mobile Phones" %}

{% trans "Mobile phones must be switched off during classes. Use is only allowed with the instructor's explicit permission." %}

{% trans "Student ID" %}

{% trans "Show your student ID when entering campus. Access is not granted without valid identification." %}

{% trans "Classroom Etiquette" %}

{% trans "Stand when the instructor enters. Do not eat, chew gum, or engage in activities unrelated to the lesson during class." %}

{% trans "Hallway Conduct" %}

{% trans "Walk on the right side, greet staff and faculty, do not speak loudly on the phone in corridors." %}

{% trans "Meetings and Events" %}

{% trans "Show respect to speakers, maintain order, and do not interrupt without the moderator's permission." %}

{% trans "The following actions are strictly prohibited on campus and may result in disciplinary action, including expulsion:" %}

  • {% trans "Cheating, plagiarism, or submitting false documents" %}
