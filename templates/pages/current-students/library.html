Your academic hub

The TIU Library is more than just a place to borrow books. It's a modern learning environment with quiet study zones, group collaboration rooms, computer stations, and access to thousands of digital resources.

Whether you're writing an essay, preparing for exams, or conducting research — the library is here to support you at every stage of your studies.

What we offer

Book lending

Borrow up to 5 books at a time for up to 14 days. Renewals available online.

Digital databases

Access JSTOR, Scopus, ProQuest, and other academic databases on and off campus.

Group study rooms

Book a private room for group projects or study sessions via the LMS portal.

Printing & scanning

Self-service printers and scanners on every floor. Pay with your student card.

Research support

Our librarians offer one-on-one sessions to help with referencing, citations, and research methods.

Extended hours

Open Monday–Saturday 8:00–21:00, Sunday 10:00–18:00. Extended hours during exam periods.

Opening hours

The library is open every day of the week. During exam periods, we extend our hours to give you extra time to prepare.
