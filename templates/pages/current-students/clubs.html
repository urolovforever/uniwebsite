Clubs & Societies

Join a student club or start your own — connect with like-minded students at TIU.

Where unconventional minds connect

A student club or society is more than just a group — it's a chance to find your people. Whether you're diving deeper into something you love or throwing yourself into something completely new, there's no easier way to meet like-minded friends and make the most of university life.

Our clubs and societies cover everything — from the academic to the creative, debate to drama, football to photography. Whatever you're into, there's a place for you here.

Heads up

You'll need to be an enrolled TIU student to join a club or society

Clubs and sports teams

Sport and creativity are a huge part of student life at TIU, and our Student Union is at the heart of it all.

With a growing number of student-led clubs, ranging from casual meetups to competitive leagues, it's the perfect way to meet new people, stay active and make unforgettable memories.

You'll be supported by the Student Union, dedicated club coordinators, and TIU staff. They will help to grow your clubs and ensure student life continues to go from strength to strength.

Start your own club or society

Don't see anything that suits you? You can set up your own club or sports team. TIU is full of different, like-minded people, and starting your own society is a great way to build your own tribe. You don't need hundreds of new members (just 3!) and sometimes it's about quality over quantity.

So if you know 3 other people who want to start a debate club, a coding society, a book circle, or anything else — you know what to do.

And they're easier to find than you think...

Student Union

Your Student Union is led by students, for students. When you enrol at TIU, you're automatically a free member of TIUSU — there's no membership fee.

We're here to make sure you feel part of a thriving community, supporting you to stand up for what you believe in, speak out on the issues that matter, and gain the skills to shape your future.

From societies and sports to social spaces, support, and representation, TIUSU is here to make your university experience unforgettable.
