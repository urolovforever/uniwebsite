University Overview

About TIU

Shape your future at a world-class institution - with innovative programs, a supportive community, and endless opportunities for growth and discovery.

One Campus. One University.
A Lifetime of Memories

When you add it all up, your time at TIU will last way into the future. From day one, you're part of a welcoming, supportive community. Where you're free to try new experiences and push yourself academically and socially.

Want to live a lot at university? Join us, and make your time count.

OUR CAMPUS

Find your haven in a sea of change

Over the years, we've evolved our campus to include everything you need to live, study and socialize. Alongside our historic buildings, tree-filled lawns and 1,000+ trees, you'll find modern structures of steel and glass. New facilities for a fresh generation of minds. Like yours.

Our Campus

ACCOMMODATION

Woke up late? You've still got time.

When you choose our on-campus accommodation, you can roll out of bed and go straight to your lectures. Imagine it: No catching the bus at the crack of dawn. No rushing across town to get on in. Just lots of lie-ins and leisurely breakfasts before you start your studies.

Accommodation

LIVING IN TASHKENT

Tashkent's full of different like-minded people

What makes a city? Its people. And ours are a breed apart. Tashkent is a haven for unconventionally brilliant minds and famously friendly Northern souls. Living in Tashkent is affordable, vibrant, and full of charm. We can't wait to introduce you to everyone. Together, we'll help you find your people.
