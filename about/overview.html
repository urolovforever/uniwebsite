About TIU

Overview

Discover Tashkent International University - a vibrant academic community, an inclusive campus environment, and a city full of opportunity for your future.

One campus. One city. A lifetime of memories

When you add it all up, your time at TIU will last way into the future. From day one, you're part of a welcoming, supportive community. Where you're free to try new experiences and push yourself academically and socially. Want to live a lot at university? Join us, and make your time count.

Our Campus

Find your haven in a sea of change

Over the years, we've evolved our campus to include everything you need to live, study and socialise. Alongside our modern buildings and green spaces, you'll find state-of-the-art facilities. New opportunities for a fresh generation of minds. Like yours.

Our Campus

Accommodation

Woke up late? You've still got time.

When you choose our on-campus accommodation, you can roll out of bed and go straight into your lectures. Imagine it. No catching the bus at the crack of dawn. No rushing across town to get to uni. Just lots of lie-ins and leisurely breakfasts before you start your studies.

Accommodation
Living in Tashkent

Tashkent is full of different like-minded people

What makes a city? Its people. And ours are a breed apart. Tashkent is a haven for unconventionally brilliant minds and famously friendly souls. Living in Tashkent is affordable, vibrant, and full of charm. We can't wait to introduce you to everyone. Together, we'll help you find your people.

Living in Tashkent
Your Student Union

Want to raise your voice? Let's find it first.

You automatically become a member of TIU Students' Union when you accept your place. So you're already part of something bigger before you even arrive. We're here to help you find your people, try new things and have your voice heard on issues that mean the most to you.
